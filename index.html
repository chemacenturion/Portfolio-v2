Bio:

Hello, my name is José María (or as we abbreviate in español: Chema). I am currently a student enrolled in UPENN's Coding Bootcamp powered by Trilogy. I am enjoying the course even more than I anticipated. After growing up in Philadelphia and attending the High School for Creative and Performing Arts (with a concentration in visual arts) I found myself unable to express my graphic arts background within the workspace. I consider myself fortunate that this avenue has come along to allow myself a new opportunity to express my creativity. If you return to the home page you will find links to my resume, my current WITB (What's In The Bag), as well as a very nice write-up about me from The Golf Association of Philadelphia when I won the Francis C. Poore Caddie of The Year Award in 2013. I first attended The Golf Academy of Orlando in 2008 and would later put myself through college as both a caddie and a golf instructor. Upon graduation from Temple University I was primarily practicing manual sports therapy full time up until the pandemic. This pandemic has forced all of the world's population to make life changes both large and small. With that said, I am extremely excited and fortunate to embark upon this new career venture into web development.
